3.1 Technical Layers

User Interaction Layer

Supports sophisticated front-end DApp interactions exclusively via mainstream decentralized wallets (e.g., MetaMask, WalletConnect). Crucially, this layer acts as the user's transparent window into their entire portfolio. It facilitates hyper-responsive real-time price tracking, historical data visualization, intuitive order placement UI, and inherently seamless cryptographic wallet integration consistently without ever requiring friction-inducing email/password logins.

Smart Contract Layer

Handles the immutable core business logic fundamentally powering the protocol, such as trading algorithms, immediate settlement workflows, and asset identity mapping. The heavily audited smart contracts permanently deployed at this critical layer encode the essential, deterministic rules of the platform.

This on-chain layer robustly executes the most critical backend logic: verifying complex ECDSA RFQ signatures, precisely executing block trades via atomic escrow, rigorously maintaining tokenized asset balances, and programmatically managing the 1:1 mapping schema between actively circulating on-chain tokens and the deeply custodied real-world offline assets.

Asset Custody & Off-Chain Service Layer

The highly regulated bridge rigorously constructed between the decentralized blockchain and the strictly regulated traditional financial world. This profoundly robust off-chain infrastructural layer actively ensures the platform's overarching stability, ongoing legality, and continuous liquidity.

  • Institutional-Grade Asset Custody: Aggressively partners with premier traditional finance mega-institutions (such as Interactive Brokers) to deploy genuinely segregated accounts securely handling underlying assets.
  • Liquidity Aggregator: Dynamically computationally integrates and structurally synchronizes high-throughput liquidity simultaneously from both native decentralized on-chain sources and our partnered, phenomenally liquid institutional brokerages. This systemic dual-source aggregation structurally ensures exceptionally deep, undeniably consistent live markets, functionally minimizing disruptive slippage.
