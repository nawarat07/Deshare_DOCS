Security Framework Overview

The Dual Protection Standard

A protocol natively handling real-world tokenized assets operates at the critical intersection of decentralized ledger technology and heavily enforced legacy financial regulation. Security therefore necessitates an uncompromising dual-layered standard.

Deshare's security framework is uncompromising. First, our On-Chain Security Posture ensures logic is devoid of single points of failure, utilizing externally audited smart contracts that simply orchestrate logic based on mathematically sound cryptographic signatures, removing all centralized treasury risk.

Second, our Off-Chain Custodial Framework utilizes isolated bankruptcy-remote vehicles held explicitly at leading tier-one global market brokerages, actively and deterministically preventing any potential co-mingling of active user capital with our own administrative operational runways.
